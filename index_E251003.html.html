What is Data Science?

Data Science is the field that combines statistics, programming, and domain knowledge to extract valuable insights from data. It is often considered the backbone of Artificial Intelligence. Without proper data analysis, organizations may make decisions based on assumptions rather than facts.

Applications of Data Science:

Some common applications include:
1.Healthcare -Predicting diseases.
2.Finance -Fraud detection.
3.Marketing - Customer behavior analysis.
4.Technology - Recommendation systems.

Important Concepts

In Data Science, we often deal with mathematical notations such as H2O (data preprocessing), and calculating growth rates like 2n (big data scalability).
